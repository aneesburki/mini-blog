Tips For a Web Dev Beginner

Learn Touch Typing

Reasons

Speed and Efficiency

You don’t want to waste time hunting for keys when coding. The faster you type, the faster you can write and debug.

Better Focus

If you don’t have to look at your keyboard, you can keep your eyes on the screen and think about the code instead.

Less Fatigue

Typing without extra effort means less stress on your fingers and mind.

How can i touch type?

Use free tools like Typing Club or Monkey Type to practice. You don’t need to be blazing fast—just comfortable!

Build Mindset

Problem Solving Approach

Coding is basically solving problems. An improved positive mindset helps you push through tough challenges.

Growth Mindset

You will make mistakes. A lot. Instead of getting frustrated, see them as learning opportunities.

Stay Curious

Ask why code works (or doesn’t).

Patience > Talent:

It’s not about being “smart”; it’s about being stubborn enough to keep trying.

Focus on One Topic
At a Time

Reasons?

Its Less Overwhelming

Learning too many things at once can be confusing and frustrating.

Better Retention

Mastering one concept before moving on helps you truly understand it.

Confidence Boost

Small wins keep you motivated, and focusing on one thing at a time helps you get those wins.

Other Skills

Knowing How to Google

Being good at searching for solutions online is an underrated but super important skill.

Writing Clean, Readable Code

Code should be understandable, not just to computers but also to other humans too XD.

Consistency is Key

Programming isn’t something you master overnight. Regular practice makes a huge difference.
